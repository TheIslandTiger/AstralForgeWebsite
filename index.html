ABOUT US

Astral Forge is made up of a team of graduates from the University of Staffordshire and we are working to create immersive story-driven worlds for our players to explore and enjoy. We are a group of friends striving to put their mark on the games industry through our passion and dedication to the craft. Our team consists of programmers, designers, animators, artists, and more who are all experts in their area and are excited to show what they can do in a professional setting.

EVENTS,

The team at Astral Forge are hard at work developing and showcasing our games to audiences.


Protoplay

The 19th-20th of September saw the team at Astral Forge attend Protoplay in Dundee to showcase Fate's Loop.

Showcasing our first game in another country was something we never expected and gave us a great insight into how people play and view our game. We are using this information to make certain changes that we are positive players will love! It was also amazing to see the games from the other Tranzfuser teams in person.


Tranzfuser

During June-September 2025 the founded members of Astral Forge took part in the Tranzfuser programme.

This is a competitive graduate grant programme where teams are given funding and advice to help build their game and studio. We used this time to begin development on Fate's Loop and start to build the company!
