ABOUT US

ABOUT US

With over 15 years in the industry, Hot Beans Web is an ever-growing studio located in England. Built out of a passion for web development, we have worked with clients from all over the globe to produce top quality, custom websites that are uniquely tailored to them. Our dedicated team work closely with each client in creating their personalised site so that we can find solutions that allow us to provide a complete solution maximising the experience, design, and overall use of the website. As a studio, we have one mission; to create the best websites possible. We want to give everyone the chance to have a website that allows themselves or their business to display their story and mission to global audiences without losing any of the attention to detail that make us who we are today.

OUR HISTORY

OUR HISTORY



Established in 2007, located in Essex (HQ) and London, England, Hot Beans Web was born out of a raw passion for web development.

The studio’s founders: Lauren D and Leah F, both pioneers in website development, decided to employ their industry experience, and in 2007, Hot Beans Web was born.
